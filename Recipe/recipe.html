Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In the meantime, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  6. Once the sauce is ready, serve over cooked spaghetti. Optionally, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Enjoy your Classic Spaghetti Bolognese!
